What is Canimus?

Canimus is a syndication format for federated music discovery. It allows:

  • Platform-agnostic music distribution
  • Direct artist-to-listener connections
  • Decentralized music streaming
  • Fair compensation for independent musicians

How to Subscribe:

https://mirlo.space/bury-the-needle/feed.json

For Developers:

The feed is discoverable via HTML <link> tags in the page head:

      type="application/canimus+json"
      href="canimus-feed.json">
